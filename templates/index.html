How it Works

Write or paste your text into the text area. Select one or more text conversion methods from the given options. Thnen Click on the "Analyze Text" button. After that a new page will open and it contains converted text. For example, if you selected "Remove Punctuations" and "Convert to Uppercase" then the analyzed text i.e. converted text will not contain any punctuation and also it will be converted to uppercase.

About Conversion methods
Remove Punctuations

It will remove all the punctuations from the entered text.

Convert to Uppercase

It will convert the whole text into Uppercase.

Newline Remover

It will remove all the new lines from the entered text.

Remove Extra Spaces

It will remove all the extra spaces from the entered text."Spaces" are all characters that look like a blank space, i.e. a space character, a tab character, a CR character, etc.
